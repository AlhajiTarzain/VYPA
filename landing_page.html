About

The inspiration for developing the Fleet Management App stems from a combination of personal experience and the desire to solve a prevalent problem in the fleet management industry. As someone who has witnessed the inefficiencies of manually managing driver and vehicle assignments, I was driven to create a solution that modernizes and streamlines this process.
